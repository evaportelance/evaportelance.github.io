Hi! I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at Mila — Québec Artificial Intelligence Institute and McGill University working with professors Timothy J. O'Donnell and Siva Reddy. I completed my Ph.D. in computational/cognitive linguistics at Stanford University, where I was co-advised by Mike C. Frank and Dan Jurafsky. My research intersects computational modeling and language acquisition, though I have also dabbled in theoretical syntax and digital humanities. I am interested in understanding how both humans and machines learn to understand and produce language.

My pronouns are she/they.
